Frequently Asked Questions

What is OnlineFootballMatch?

Online Football Match is a free 2D memory game where players match football players to their clubs. You can play solo, with a friend, or against the computer. The game features various grid sizes and includes bonus "magic cards" for extra points!

How do I play the game?

Click the "Play" button on the PLAY NOW Page. Choose your game mode:

  • Solo : play alone and try to get the highest score.
  • With Friend : two players take turns and compete for the best score.
  • Against Computer : play against an AI opponent (Easy, Medium, or Hard).

Then select your grid size: "4x4", "4x5", or "4x6".

How do I score points?
  • Match a player with their club → 1 point
  • Match a pair of magic cards → 2 points
  • Each match adds to your total score.
What are magic cards?

Magic cards are special cards that appear once per game. If you match both magic cards, you earn 2 bonus points. Only one pair of magic cards appears in each game.

Can clubs or players repeat in a game?

No. In each game:

  • Only one player per club is selected.
  • No repeated clubs or players.
  • Magic card appears only once per game.
What devices can I play on?

The game works on desktop, tablet, and mobile browsers. For the best experience, we recommend playing on desktop with full screen.

Do I need to create an account to play?

No, the game is free to play with no account or sign-in required.

Is there a time limit?

Each game has a timer that tracks how long you play — but there’s no time limit. Try to finish the game as fast as you can to beat your own time!

Is this game free?

Yes! Online Football Match is completely free to play and always will be.

Is my data collected?

We use cookies only to improve your experience and comply with Google AdSense and EU regulations. For more info, see our Terms.
